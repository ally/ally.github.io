I help conceptualise, facilitate, design, prototype and build digital products. I've worked with agencies and international brands, as well as NGOs and non-profits to create useful solutions to complex problems. I have a particular interest in designing for novice tech users and people in emerging economies. If you need help figuring out your product, say hello.
